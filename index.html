Little Diary Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 19, 2026

Overview

Little Diary is a private family diary app for recording and preserving memories of your children. We take your family's privacy seriously.

What we collect

  • Account information — your email address and display name when you sign up
  • Voice recordings — audio you record within the app
  • Transcripts — text generated from your voice recordings via OpenAI Whisper
  • Photos — images you choose to attach to diary entries
  • Child information — your child's name and birthdate

Who can see your data

Your diary is completely private. Only family members you explicitly invite can see your child's entries. We do not sell, share, or monetize your data in any way.

Third-party services

  • Supabase — stores your data securely (supabase.com/privacy)
  • OpenAI — transcribes your voice recordings. Audio is sent to OpenAI for processing and is subject to OpenAI's privacy policy (openai.com/privacy). OpenAI does not use API-submitted audio to train its models.

Data retention

Your data is stored until you delete your account. Deleting your account permanently removes all your data including recordings, transcripts, and photos.

Children's privacy

Little Diary is intended for use by adults to record memories about their children. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. The app is operated by parents and family members, not by children themselves.
